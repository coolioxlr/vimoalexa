Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Vimo uses cookies to help Vimo identify and track visitors, their usage of Vimo website, and their website access preferences. Vimo visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Vimo’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Vimo’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
